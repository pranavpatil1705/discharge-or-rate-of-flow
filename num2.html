RECTANGULAR NOTCH FORMULA

Q = \(\frac{8}{15}C_d\tan\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)\sqrt{2g}H^{3/2}\)

  • Q = Discharge (cubic feet per second)
  • Cd = Discharge Coefficient
  • \(\theta\) = Notch Angle (degrees)
  • H = Head (feet)
  • g = Acceleration due to Gravity (ft/s²)
