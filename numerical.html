MANNING'S FORMULA

Q = A /n * R2/3 * S1/2

  • Q = Discharge (cu. ft./sec.)
  • A = Cross-sectional Area of Flow (sq. ft.)
  • n = Coefficient of Roughness
  • R = Hydraulic Radius (ft.)
  • S = Slope of Pipe (ft./ft.)

Hydraulic Radius

R = A / P

  • R = Hydraulic Radius (ft.)
  • A = Cross-sectional Area of Flow (sq. ft.)
  • P = Wetted perimeter (ft.)
